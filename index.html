Top 5 Places to Visit in Switzerland

Switzerland tourist attraction guide:

#1. Bernina Express :

The Bernina Express is known for its amazing views on the renowned, year-round panoramic train operating between Chur/St. Moritz (Switzerland) and Tirano (Italy). It traverses the highest railway across the Alps. The route covers about approximately 148 km (92 miles) in about 4 hours, linking diverse languages and cultures. Some higlights to look forward to if going to this attraction is the Brusio Spiral Viaduct, Landwasser Viaduct, and views of glaciers and palm trees. Overall a very beautiful place to visit.

#2. Interlaken:

Interlaken is a traditional resort town in the mountainous Bernese Oberland region of central Switzerland. Interlaken is the home of skydiving, kayaking, paragliding, and any other outdoor activity Europe has to offer. With its stunning views and beautiful water, it makes it easy to spend time outdoors. One of the main reasons to visit Interlaken is its breathtaking scenery. The town sits in a valley that offers panoramic views of snow-capped mountains and crystal-clear lakes.

#3. Schilthorn Piz Gloria (restraunt)

Let's take a break from exploring and take a look at some restraunts, shall we! The Schilthorn Piz Gloria is a famous 360-degree revolving restaurant located at the 2,970-meter (9,744 ft) summit of the Schilthorn mountain in the Bernese Alps, Switzerland. The site offers a Skyline View Platform and has a viewing deck also offering panoramic views of the Swiss Alps.It takes you just 32 minutes by Cable car to reach the summit of the 2970 m high Schilthorn. Enjoy the beautiful view of Eiger, Monch and Jungfrau while having your meal!

#4. lindt Home of chocolate

Switzerland's ultimate chocolate attraction. The Lindt Home of Chocolate is a state-of-the-art experience for chocolate lovers.The Lindt Home of Chocolate is a chocolate museum, research center, and visitor attraction located in Kilchberg.

#5. The Park Hotel Viznauz

Park Hotel Vitznau is celebrated by many travelers for its high-end, elegant experience and exceptional value, with the wine cellar tour frequently noted as a highlight.
